# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience.

## Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainer. All complaints will be reviewed promptly and fairly.

Maintainer will preserve confidentiality to the extent possible.
